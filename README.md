Gameplay:

- You have 3 lives and when you hit your enemy he loses one. The first to defeat the enemy wins

Controls:
 
 - A & D: Move right and left
 
 - MOUSE: With the mouse you can put direction and power

 - SPACE: Shoot
  
 - Others:

   F1: show/hide colliders
